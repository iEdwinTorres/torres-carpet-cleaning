About

One day in 1988 in Santa Ana, California. One man with a beat up old truck found himself looking at several pieces of carpet cleaning equipment at a local thrift shop that were about as beat up as his truck. With very little money, and a whole lot of entrepreneurial spirit. He took a risk that eventually saw him traveling all of southern California. Cleaning bedrooms and living rooms in hundreds of homes and hundreds of offices and restaraunts as well. Thirty years later he still has that passion and drive to leave their customers 100% satisfied.
